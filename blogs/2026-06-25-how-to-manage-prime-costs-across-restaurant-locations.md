Why Prime Cost is the number that matters most If you're running multiple restaurant locations, you already know that your profit lives or dies by one number: prime cost. It's the combination of your food costs, beverage costs and labor costs, and for most restaurants, it eats up somewhere between 55% and 65% of every dollar you bring in.
